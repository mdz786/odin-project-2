This Website is about the Greatness of Islam

Islam is an Abrahamic monotheistic religion centred primarily around the Quran, a religious text considered by Muslims to be the direct word of God as it was revealed to Muhammad, the main and final Islamic prophet(P.B.U.H).

Holy places in Islam

Al-Kaʿbah al-Musharrafah is a building at the center of Islam's most important mosque, the Masjid al-Haram in Mecca, Saudi Arabia.

Al-Masjid an-Nabawi Its striking Green Dome rises above the tombs of the Prophet Muhammad and early Islamic leaders Abu Bakr and Umar.

Jami' Al-Aqsa is a congregational mosque located in the Old City of Jerusalem.
